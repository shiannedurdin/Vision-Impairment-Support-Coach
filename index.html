Shianne Durdin Vision Support Coach

Description

As a child, I was fortunate to have attended a blind school where I received support in various aspects of life with low vision such as assistive technology, braille courses and adaptive sports, however, I always lacked the comfort of having people I could talk to, who had the lived experience of a blind or low vision individual. And that is why as a support coach for the vision impaired, I not only offer personalised braille and screen reader training, tailored advice on pathways for the future, and strategies for a more independent daily life, I also offer mentorship. A space of understanding. A place where each and every one of my clients feels heard and validated. Because while blindness might mean lack of sight, it does not mean lack of vision. Online platform Zoom will be used for all appointments and programs.

Programs

  • 6 week introduction to navigating a computer using Jaws — One-on-one online program with weekly 45 minute sessions. Total cost: AUD $280, due before the beginning of the second session. Content includes navigating File Explorer and Word documents, creating and saving files, connecting to internet and Bluetooth devices, configuring settings, accessing the internet via Google Chrome, JAWS-specific shortcuts, and anything the client would like to learn.
  • 6 week introduction to using iPad and iPhone with VoiceOver — One-on-one online program with weekly 45 minute sessions. Total cost: AUD $300, due before the beginning of the third session. Content includes sending and replying to emails and messages, making calls, configuring settings, navigating websites and well-known apps, navigating documents, and anything the client would like to learn.
  • 8 week Exploring Pathways — Weekly 45 minute sessions. Total cost: AUD $300, due by the beginning of the third session. Content includes daily living skills, goal setting, peer mentoring and emotional support. The first session explores your current, short-term and long-term goals as an individual with blindness or low vision; each appointment begins with a check-in on what you need for the session.
  • 4 week introduction to Basic Braille — One-on-one online program with weekly 45 minute sessions. Total cost: AUD $200, due before the beginning of the second session. Content includes the braille alphabet, basic maths symbols, and anything else the client would like to learn. Braille paper and a braille machine are required. Additional learning resources will be sent via post.
  • 6 Week Introduction To Braille Music — One-on-one online program with weekly 45 minute sessions. Total cost: AUD $330, due before the beginning of the second session. Content includes basic notation and rhythm, writing scales in braille, composing simple melodies, and anything else the client would like to learn. Braille paper and a braille machine are required. Additional learning resources will be sent via post.
