Shianne Durdin Vision Support Coach

Description

As a child, I was fortunate to have attended a blind school where I received support in various aspects of life with low vision such as assistive technology, braille courses and adaptive sports, however, I always lacked the comfort of having people I could talk to, who had the lived experience of a blind or low vision individual. And that is why as a support coach for the vision impaired, I not only offer personalised braille and screen reader training, tailored advice on pathways for the future, and strategies for a more independent daily life, I also offer mentorship. A space of understanding. A place where each and every one of my clients feels heard and validated. Because while blindness might mean lack of sight, it does not mean lack of vision. Online platform Zoom will be used for all appointments and programs.

Programs

  • 6 week introduction to navigating a computer using Jaws

    One-on-one online program. Weekly 45 minute sessions.

    Total cost: AUD $280. Due before the beginning of the second session.

    Topics: File Explorer, Word documents, creating and saving files, internet and Bluetooth connections, settings, Google Chrome, JAWS shortcuts, plus anything the client would like to learn.

  • 6 week introduction to using iPad and iPhone with VoiceOver

    One-on-one online program. Weekly 45 minute sessions.

    Total cost: AUD $300. Due before the beginning of the third session.

    Topics: emails and messages, calls, settings, navigating websites and well-known apps, navigating documents, plus anything the client would like to learn.

  • 8 week Exploring Pathways

    Weekly 45 minute sessions.

    Total cost: AUD $300. Due by the beginning of the third session.

    Includes daily living skills, goal setting, peer mentoring and emotional support. The first session explores your current, short-term and long-term goals. Each appointment starts with a check-in.

  • 4 week introduction to Basic Braille

    Weekly 45 minute sessions.

    Total cost: AUD $200. Due before the second session.

    Covers braille alphabet, basic maths symbols, plus anything the client would like to learn. Requires braille paper and machine. Additional resources sent via post.

  • 6 Week Introduction To Braille Music

    Weekly 45 minute sessions.

    Total cost: AUD $330. Due before the second session.

    Covers notation and rhythm, writing scales in braille, composing simple melodies, plus anything else the client would like to learn. Requires braille paper and machine. Resources sent via post.

  • 6 Week Intermediate iPad and iPhone Navigation Using VoiceOver

    One-on-one online program.

    Total cost: AUD $330. Due before the second session.

    Topics: VoiceOver shortcuts, downloading attachments, sending emails and messages with attachments, installing Be My Eyes and Seeing AI, Braille Screen Input mode, plus anything the client would like.

  • 6 Week Intermediate Braille Music

    Weekly 1 hour sessions.

    Total cost: AUD $350. Due before the third session.

    Topics: writing chords, composing piano music for both hands, key and time signatures, syncopated rhythms, piano score layout, repeats and dynamic markings, plus anything the client would like. Requires braille paper and machine. Resources sent via post.

  • 6 Week Intermediate Braille

    Weekly 1 hour sessions.

    Total cost: AUD $300. Due before the second session.

    Topics: contractions (single dot combinations used for whole words), punctuation, intermediate maths symbols, brief history of braille, plus anything the client would like. Requires braille machine and paper. Resources sent via post.

Every program can be modified to meet the needs of the client.

If you want other topics such as sports for the blind, orientation and mobility, or services for daily independence, you can book individual sessions separate from any program.
