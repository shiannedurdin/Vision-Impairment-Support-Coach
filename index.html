Description

As a child, I was fortunate to have attended a blind school where I received support in various aspects of life with low vision such as assistive technology, braille courses and adaptive sports, however, I always lacked the comfort of having people I could talk to, who had the lived experience of a blind or low vision individual. And that is why as a support coach for the vision impaired, I not only offer personalised braille and screen reader training, tailored advice on pathways for the future, and strategies for a more independent life, but also a safe, supportive space where lived experience leads the way.

What I offer

• Braille and screen reader coaching (JAWS, NVDA, VoiceOver) • Accessible tech skills • Study and work readiness • Daily living strategies • Sports and mobility encouragement • Parent/ally guidance.
