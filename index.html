Shianne Durdin Vision Support Coach

Description

As a child, I was fortunate to have attended a blind school where I received support in various aspects of life with low vision such as assistive technology, braille courses and adaptive sports, however, I always lacked the comfort of having people I could talk to, who had the lived experience of a blind or low vision individual.

And that is why as a support coach for the vision impaired, I not only offer personalised braille and screen reader training, tailored advice on pathways for the future, and strategies for a more independent daily life, I also offer mentorship. A space of understanding. A place where each and every one of my clients feels heard and validated.

Because while blindness might mean lack of sight, it does not mean lack of vision. Online platform Zoom will be used for all appointments and programs, all of which are designed to cater to blind and low vision individuals, as well as parents and allies.

Programs

  • 6 Week Introduction to Navigating a Computer Using Jaws
    Weekly 45 minute sessions. Cost: AUD $300 due before the second session.
    Covers file explorer, Word documents, creating and saving files, connecting to internet and Bluetooth devices, configuring settings, accessing the internet via Google Chrome, Jaws shortcuts, and client-requested topics.
  • 6 Week Introduction to Using iPad and iPhone with Voice Over
    Weekly 45 minute sessions. Cost: AUD $300 due before the third session.
    Covers sending and replying to emails and messages, making calls, configuring settings, navigating websites and apps, navigating documents, and client-requested topics.
  • 8 Week Exploring Pathways
    Weekly 45 minute sessions. Cost: AUD $300 due before the third session.
    Covers daily living skills, goal setting, peer mentoring, emotional support, and ongoing goal check-ins.
  • 4 Week Introduction to Basic Braille
    Weekly 45 minute sessions. Cost: AUD $230 due before the second session.
    Covers braille alphabet, basic Maths symbols, and client-requested topics. Requires braille paper and braille machine. Additional resources sent via post.
  • 6 Week Introduction to Braille Music
    Weekly 45 minute sessions. Cost: AUD $350 due before the second session.
    Covers basic notation, rhythm, scales, simple melodies, and client-requested topics. Requires braille paper and braille machine. Resources sent via post.
  • 6 Week Intermediate iPad and iPhone Navigation Using Voice Over
    Cost: AUD $330 due before the second session.
    Covers Voice Over shortcuts, downloading attachments, sending attachments, using apps Be My Eyes and Seeing AI, braille screen input mode, and client-requested topics.
  • 6 Week Intermediate Braille Music
    Weekly 1 hour sessions. Cost: AUD $390 due before the third session.
    Covers writing chords in braille, composing piano music, key and time signatures, syncopated rhythms, score layout, repeats, dynamics, and client-requested topics. Requires braille machine and paper. Resources sent via post.
  • 6 Week Intermediate Braille
    Weekly 1 hour sessions. Cost: AUD $320 due before the second session.
    Covers braille contractions, punctuation, intermediate Maths symbols, braille history, and client-requested topics. Requires braille machine and paper. Resources sent via post.

Every program can be modified to meet the needs of the client. Individual sessions are also available.
